How does shipping work?
As soon as you purchase a paperback, we begin printing (usually 3-5 days) then ship to your address through USPS media mail (4-14 days). Shipping and handling fees are included in the button price above. If you'd like a copy shipped somewhere outside of the U.S. or Canada, please contact us.
Refunds and returns?
Your purchase is non-refundable and non-returnable. We do not have the capacity to support such services and guarantees. That said, we always try take responsibility above and beyond what a reasonable person might expect. Please contact us if you have any problems, concerns, feedback, or happy fun whimsical notes and stories that let us know we're doing something right. "I would rather return my arm than your book because it's so great!"
What if I can't afford a book?
We share valuable ideas by empowering folks to take initiative. Our mission is in our name! Let us know your circumstances, and we'll do our best to remove lack of resources as an obstacle to access.
Why not Amazon?
We are a small company, dedicated to our customers and authors. Amazon puts up a barrier between us and you while taking away profits. We decided to sacrifice a bit of convenience to enable a more humane, personal, and caring business. We're excited to connect with you directly and proud to help our authors create and share great work! Thanks for your support, and do let us know your feedback if you need a book to be on Amazon. (Our authors net over 30% more per book with us vs Amazon.)
Any resources for aspiring authors?
First, write your book. Open up a document and just type—until you have a finished first draft. Then get in touch, and we’ll help as best we can! For a more detailed process walk-through, read this author's article.
